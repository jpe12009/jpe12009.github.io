CASTLE DEFENSE

- Attention General -


We have been at war with the abominations for several weeks.
With your strategic expertise we may be able to defeat them once and for all! Let's discuss our approach...


Objective: Reduce your opponent's life to 0


------------------ Round structure -------------------


  • 1. Buy units at the market. You are given gold at the start of each round.

  • 2. You attack first. Battle begins with the first unit you purchased.

  • 3. Your unit continues attacking until either it misses, the defending unit survives the attack, or when no enemies remain.

  • 4. If the round is not over, the enemy attacks in the same manner.

  • 5. Whoever destroys their opponent's army for the round is the round winner!

  • 6. The victor deals damage to the opposing castle equal to the sum of their units' attack stat.

  • 7. The game board is cleared and then the enemy repopulates the game board with a stronger army.

  • Familiarize yourself with the enemy's forces so you can purchase units accordingly at the start of a new round.
    Or you can just click a bunch and hope for the best.

  • Have fun!

    • After clicking the button below follow the instructions in the white box to build your army.
